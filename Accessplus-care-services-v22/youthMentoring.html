Youth Mentoring

Mentoring / Services / Training Service

Helping young people live full and healthy lives with Youth Mentoring

Our mentoring programs provides Grace Care Services with the opportunity to increase our service responsiveness. Developing a positive and trusting relationship with a mentor can help young people on the road to recovery. Our Mentoring Program supports young people aged 12 to 25 who are experiencing early signs associated with suicide, depression, anxiety, and self-harm and are accessing support services from the NDIS through Grace Care Service.

How Our Youth Mentoring Services Proved to Be Best for You?

Throughout the course of the program period, we aim to achieve the following;

  • To engage in prosocial and recreational activities
  • To remain engaged with support services.
  • To complete mental health assessment and engage
    in mental health support services
  • Support to reconnect with family
  • To make positive choices
  • To return to school or find employment
  • Reduce offending behaviours

We operates a free, face-to-face and web-based professional counselling service for young people aged 12 to 25 who may be experiencing mental health challenges including suicidal thoughts, depression, anxiety and self-harm.
